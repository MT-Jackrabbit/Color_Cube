GAME PLAY

The up, left, right and down buttons located above the cube spin the cube in the corresponsing direction. You can also use the arrow keys on the keyboard to spin the cube.

You click on the outside edges of the corner squares to spin the columns (up/down) or the rows (left/right). The center column and row DO NOT spin.

To spin the FRONT FACE click on the arrows in the center of the cube to spin face right or left.

To spin the BACK FACE you use the 'B' key to spin it left, ATL+B will spin the face right.
